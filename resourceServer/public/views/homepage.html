How to Play

  1. Each player is dealt two cards.
  2. The goal is to get a hand total as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.
  3. Players can choose to "hit" (draw another card) or "stand" (keep the current hand).
  4. If the total goes over 21, it's a "bust" and the player loses.
  5. The player with the highest total not exceeding 21 wins.
